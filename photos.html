Artist Statement
Photos My work aims to communicate the beauty of the world no matter where you are looking at it from.
I chose to take a photography class to further accomplish that vision.
The biggest similarities in my work exist in the vibrancy and contrast of every image.
I believe that a colorful image serves to further my goal because
bright colors draw attention, which is critical to my goal.
Although I shoot anything, portraits are my favorite because
I believe that a person's face can tell a better story than any still object ever could.
My favorite artist to emulate is Yousuf Karsh because of the bright whites and deep blacks in his work,
which bring to light a big story. From my photos I want others to see the
world for how beautiful it can truly be and that even the gloomiest of photos can have light.
